Return & Refund Policy

Effective Date: May 23, 2025

At The Cake Shop, every dessert is handmade with care and attention to detail. Because our products are perishable and custom-made, we have specific guidelines for returns and refunds.

Custom Orders & Cakes

  • All custom orders are non-refundable once production has started.
  • If there is an issue with your order, please contact us within 24 hours of pick-up or delivery so we can evaluate and address the concern.
  • We do not accept returns on custom cakes or desserts unless there was a clear error on our part (e.g., wrong design, flavor, or product).

Pre-Orders & Cancellations

  • Cancellations must be made at least 48 hours in advance for a full refund.
  • Cancellations within 48 hours of pickup/delivery are non-refundable.

In-Store Purchases

  • Due to health and safety regulations, all baked goods are final sale once they leave our store.
  • If you're unsatisfied with your in-store purchase, please bring the item back the same day with proof of purchase so we can evaluate the issue.

Contact Us

We want every experience to be a sweet one. If something isn’t right, please contact us at thecakeshop@example.com or call (760) 946-5500, and we’ll do our best to make it right.
